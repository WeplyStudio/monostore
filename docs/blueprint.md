# **App Name**: MonoStore

## Core Features:

- Product Browsing: Browse and filter a variety of digital assets including icons, templates, and e-books.
- Search Functionality: Find specific digital assets by name or category using the search bar.
- Product Detail View: View detailed information about a selected product, including description, features, and price.
- Shopping Cart: Add and manage selected products in a shopping cart for purchase.
- Checkout Process: Complete the purchase process with secure payment and order confirmation.
- Personalized Recommendations: AI-powered recommendations of relevant products based on browsing history and purchase patterns, acting as a product discovery tool.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Midnight blue (#2C3E50) to convey trustworthiness and sophistication in the digital marketplace.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F5F7FA) for a clean, uncluttered shopping environment.
- Accent color: Coral (#FF7F50) for call-to-action buttons and highlighting special offers to grab attention.
- Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif, for a modern and readable interface.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, for headlines and short amounts of body text.
- Use minimalistic icons from Lucide React to represent various actions and categories.
- Maintain a clean and intuitive layout with clear sections for product browsing, details, and checkout.
